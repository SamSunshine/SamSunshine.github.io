NFT Marketplace

Blockchain Technology Development

An integrated online marketplace to exclusively help individuals/organizations create/buy/sell and trade science and technology non-fungible tokens(NFTs).

Problem statement




Acquiring funding and gaining intellectual ownership is often challenging for scientists, and financing through traditional channels usually takes a long time.

The solution




We launched the first non-fungible token (NFT) marketplace exclusively for science and technology intellectual property(IPs) in March 2022. The marketplace integrates with RMDS Lab's global ecosystem connecting innovators with related investors, collectors, and science lovers. It empowers the scientific community to receive funding and establish intellectual property ownership.

Key highlights:

✓ Integrated with RMDS Lab's existing social network platform, GRMDS
✓ NFTs created on RMDS NFT can be sold on mainstream platforms such as Opensea
✓ Members can turn 35+ unique file types into NFTs
✓ Built to support multi-blockchain
