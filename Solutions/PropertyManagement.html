Home Sweet Management

Software Development

A management software to help landlords operate their residential rental properties.

Problem statement



Managing residential rental properties is a time-consuming and challenging undertaking. Deadlines, maintenance requests, home inspections, and tenant communications can fill up a property manager's day, leaving no time for other tasks.

The solution



We launched the property management software in service to Home Sweet Management operations in early April 2023. The software benefits landlords struggling to keep track of receipts, collect rent, issue late fees, and showcase available properties.

Key highlights:

✓ Built to manage finances
✓ Property maintenance email alerts
✓ Unique marketing section to attract good tenants
✓ Business performance analytics
